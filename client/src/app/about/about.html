Forcite Helmet Systems is a fast growing tech company driving design and engineering innovation for conventional safety products, particularly light recreational sport helmets. The team at Forcite consists of talented and multidisciplinary professionals, dedicated to the delivery of worldclass products.

Forcite Story

Founded in September 2013 by Alfred Boyadgis, Forcite Helmet Systems began as a research project at the University of New South Wales, Australia. The initial project was a concept developed to streamline the communication systems currently issued to Motorcycle Emergency Services.

Upon receiving mass media attention internationally and in Australia in 2012, major awards were given to this project including the Silver placement in the 2013 Dyson Innovation Awards and the Red Dot International Design Award 2013.
